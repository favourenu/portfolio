How to play

  • Under the black box is a photo
  • Guess which one is it from the list of those photos
  • Click your guess to find out if you correct or wrong
  • if you're wrong, your chances(game life) reduce
  • And if your guess is correct, you get a pop up that says correct, click the correct to continue the game because you can only win if you guess 3 correctly
  • And there's a show button that helps you see what under the black box, but you can only use it once in the game.

    • Good luck!
