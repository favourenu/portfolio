How to play:

  • Under the black box is a picture.
  • Guess which one is it from those visible pictures and click it.
  • For each wrong guess, your chances reduce (from 10 to 0).
  • If your guess is correct, your correct number increase (from 0 to 3).
  • Once your chances hit 0, you'll lose the game and if your corect guesses hit 3, you'll win.
  • The show button helps you unconver the black box and see the picture
    under it, but you can only use it once in the game.
